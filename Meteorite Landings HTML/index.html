Project Overview

This final project with data provided by the Nasa public data center shows the Meteorit that have fallen and landed on earth.

Meteorite Landings

This visualization is to analyze the meteorite and there landing from the past 220 pluss years, to try and identify patterns in their occurrences. Meteorites are solid pieces of debris from objects that originate in outer space and have passed through our atmosphere. This visualization helps display the meteorite landings over time.

Note: Only recordered meteorite landings are shown based on the Nasa public data base. Meteorite landing clusters that landed at the same location and at the same time have been filtered to appear as a single landing for accuracy and visibility.
